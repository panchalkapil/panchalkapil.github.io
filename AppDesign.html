Things to consider while designing mobile app to enhance customer experience.

Now-a-days people are more inclined towards mobile apps rather than switching to multiple websites, accessing them through credentials all the time through web browsers. With the absolute certainty of time-consuming and high data usage, it is bound to say that Continuous browsing makes people tedious. So to overcome such issues, apps were designed. These apps were designed in such a way that it fascinates the user to use it, interact it in freely and friendly manner.

Designing a Mobile App:


Designing of an app should be done by keeping focus on User-mentality. The apps designed must be compatible with the devices on which they are being run on. In order to make the app succeed, it should satisfy the needs of customer. Hundreds of apps enter in the market but only few will get best-selling success. Being a designer you should be able to attract the attention of the users through the app and make their mobile experience pleasant and easy.


The following are few things to keep in mind while designing and marketing a Mobile app :

  • Keep CSS and markups simple to achieve cross-browser compatibility for the App. Because it might be possible that few browsers may not support complex features.
  • The app should be error-free while running. Remember users are intolerant to errors and starts focusing on other apps for their business.
  • Reward the customers - for using the app, for sign up, purchasing through your app, etc. This will definitely increase your Application usage, customer counts and enhances customer experience.
  • User suggestions and their feedback should be preferred with priority to get in touch with their requirements any time.
  • Specific offers should be offered to the customers to attract their attention like providing one month free premium trial, discounts on Premium purchase, etc.

All the above points are to be considered through customer's vision to enhance the customer experience.

Why Customer experience is a key for gaining Success in Business?

Customer satisfaction is always been a major factor for business success and will always be. It is very much important to look yourself through the Customer point of view instead of moving through Business point of view. The saying is very simple that if you have understood the requirements, expectations of the customer then note it that you have won half of the battle from your end.

Customer satisfaction, subjectively a very difficult thing to measure but achieving the feedbacks from them can make us easy to improve the operations of product or App.

Avoid causing Bad Customer Experiences.

Most customers are fast operators. They always try to use your product in a smooth way and if in case faces any long wait times while operating, then they will find a way to switch into another product. This ultimately decreases your customers and eventually makes loss to your end. Therefore make sure you overcome long wait times in the product functionality.

And also ensure that you have resolved the pending bugs in the application and fixed the issues flawlessly that are being araised from the previous customers.
